In a Nutshell

Saatva is a luxury mattress brand sold exclusively online for reasonable prices. As a innerspring mattress, Saatva is made with individually wrapped coils, edge & lumbar support, steel coil support base, organic cotton cover, and euro pillow top with a memory foam enhancement in the lumbar area. Pick your comfort level between Luxury Firm, Plush Soft and Firm. Saatva offer free nationwide white glove delivery with home setup and free mattress removal, as well as a full 120 night risk free trial and 15 years warranty.
